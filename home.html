Why Choose Us?
Choosing from the many insurance companies and agencies available and determining the right amount of coverage to protect your family and your assets can be daunting. As your independent agency, The Insurax is your neighborhood agency that will help you wade through the many options and select products that are tailored to your needs and that don't leave you either underinsured or over-insured.
We are dedicated to providing the highest quality customer service. You can rest "insured" knowing that your personal agent will be looking out for you when you need them.
Our work does not end when a policy is issued; but rather, we work continuously to provide you with personal assistance on any insurance needs you may have such as Certificates of Insurance, Claims or Changes to your policy/policies.
We make it our business to give our customers peace of mind by making their insurance experience easy and convenient.

How We Are Best
At The Insurax Insurance Agency, you will find fast, accurate quotes. As independent agents, we are able to shop for comparative pricing. This allows us to locate the best rate and the best company tailored for our clients' individual needs. In truth, we feel one of the best aspects of the insurance business is the fact that rates are set by the insurance carriers. As an agent, there is no up selling a certain price or negotiating one down. We work to find the best rate, and even more importantly, the best company to place your interest with. However, the fact that these rates are set allows us to sell the most important quality we can, our service. Your insurance holdings are sometimes not given the thought they most certainly need, until the time comes when truly needed. We will be accessible to you. Your phone calls will be taken and messages returned promptly. We want to maintain a relationship with our clients.
